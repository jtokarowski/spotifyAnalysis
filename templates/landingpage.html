Music in Context

Discover Music in Context

A project by John Tokarowski

Beta Functionality: the model will retrieve the user's top recent tracks and build a pool of recommendations. The model will then fit the recommendations to a 'curve' and present the complete set as a playlist in Spotify

This tool will ask you for Spotify Authentication to create new playlists. No playlists will be deleted.
